MEET OUR TEAM

With a strong focus on integrity and transparency, the design of the item is of equal importance to the conditions under which it was created. Challenging the traditional retail model, we believe in the power of buying less by buying better. We cordially invite you to explore our collection of homewares, heirloom objects made to transcend time and trend. Column A was founded in the spring of 2020 by partners in love and life, Claire and Luke Le. Based in Sydney, they bring together extensive experience across creative disciplines and retail.

Claire has experience working in multi-brand stores, buying and trend forecasting, complemented by Luke's background in furniture making and photography/art. Together they share a deft eye for sourcing unique products for the home, driven by a deep appreciation for form-meets-function design.
